### Full Description
Ubuncare is a calm, private space to check in with yourself every day.

No account. No tracking. No data ever leaves your device. Just open the app and start.

── WHAT YOU CAN DO ──

🧠 Mood Check-In
A guided daily check-in that explores how you're feeling and what might be behind it. Honest, gentle, judgment-free.

💬 Mood Chat
Reflect on your day with your wellness guide. Short, unhurried conversations designed to help you slow down and think.

🌬️ Guided Breathing
Box breathing and 4-7-8 techniques guided step by step. Works in under 3 minutes.

🧘 Quick Calm
One tap into a 60-second breathing reset — for moments when you need to slow down right now.

🆘 Crisis Support
UK helplines always one tap away. Samaritans, NHS 111, Shout, and emergency services clearly signposted.

── AND MORE ──

• Gratitude journal
• Body scan
• 5-4-3-2-1 sensory grounding
• Emergency calm sequence
• Self-care ideas
• Weekly intention setting
• Mood history and streak tracking

── YOUR WELLNESS GUIDE ──

Choose from four guides — Amani, Kora, Nova, or Zuri — each with their own style and tone. Switch any time from Settings.

── PRIVATE BY DESIGN ──

Everything you do in Ubuncare stays on your device. Your moods, conversations, and reflections are never uploaded, shared, or sold. There are no servers. No account is ever created. This is your space.

── NOT A CLINICAL SERVICE ──

Ubuncare is a personal wellbeing tool, not a therapy service or medical device. If you are in crisis, please contact emergency services (999), Samaritans (116 123), or text SHOUT to 85258.

Free. No account. No tracking. Your space for calm and clarity.
